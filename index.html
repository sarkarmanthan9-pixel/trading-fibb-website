Glossary

SMC Key Terms

Master the language of Smart Money before diving into the charts.

Order Block (OB)

The last bearish or bullish candle before a significant institutional move — acts as S/R zone where large orders were originally placed.

Fair Value Gap (FVG)

Price imbalance created by fast impulsive moves. Price tends to return to fill these gaps before continuing the trend.

Liquidity

Stop-loss clusters above swing highs (buy-side) or below swing lows (sell-side). Institutions hunt these before reversing direction.

BOS — Break of Structure

When price creates a new HH (bullish) or LL (bearish), confirming continuation of the prevailing trend direction.

CHoCH — Change of Character

First signal of a potential trend reversal. Price breaks the opposite structure, indicating a possible directional shift.

Premium / Discount Zones

Above 50% of a swing range is premium (expensive), below is discount (cheap). Institutional bias: buy discount, sell premium.
