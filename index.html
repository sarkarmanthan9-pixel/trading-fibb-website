Glossary

SMC Key Terms

🟩
Order Block (OB)The last bearish/bullish candle before a significant move — acts as a support/resistance zone where institutions placed large orders.
🔲
Fair Value Gap (FVG)An imbalance in price created by fast moves. Price tends to revisit these zones to rebalance before continuing its trend.
💧
LiquidityStop-loss clusters above swing highs (buy-side) or below swing lows (sell-side). Institutions hunt these levels before reversing.
📊
BOS — Break of StructureWhen price creates a new higher high (in uptrend) or lower low (in downtrend), confirming continuation of trend.
🔄
CHoCH — Change of CharacterFirst sign of a potential trend reversal. Price breaks opposite structure, signaling a shift in market direction.
📐
Premium / Discount ZonesUsing the 50% level of a price range — above 50% is premium (expensive), below is discount (cheap). Buy in discount, sell in premium.
